How do I start a card program with Peppermint?

Launching a card program with Peppermint is easy.

Simply sign up, complete a quick onboarding, and then the magic begins.

Choose between virtual and/or physical cards, customize balance and transaction limits, explore card designs – build an experience fit for your business’ unique objectives right from the dashboard.

Cards will be delivered to you and your customers via dispatch or API 😎. Then sit back, relax, and enjoy higher sales, happier customers, and more control over your business.

How can Peppermint help me grow my revenue and business?

Imagine getting back all those declined payments? The reversed transfers? With Peppermint, you will. Accept payments even when devices are offline and never lose a sale again.

Enjoy faster transaction times and higher revenue with contactless payments – your customers simply tap their phone or card and go 💸.

Don’t underestimate the value of having a branded card in your customers’ hands, pockets, and hearts. Go from a basic commodity to an iconic brand 💅🏾.

As if that wasn’t enough. Leverage rich data to identify and sell more of what people want. Go a step further and direct customer behavior – add loyalty and rewards to an existing card program to give and get more from your customers.

How do I accept payments from Peppermint cards?

You can accept payments from Peppermint cards right from your NFC-enabled phone!

Once your business is verified, simply download the Peppermint merchant app on the relevant phones, connect your business profile, configure your account securely, and start enjoying the lighter, faster, and more affordable payment acceptance option.

How long does it take to get my settlements with Peppermint?

Funds are available to you as soon as customers make payments – you choose the interval at which you’d like to access them.

Enjoy same-day bulk settlements or make it instant, because why not?

You’re in control with Peppermint.

How quickly are refunds and withdrawals processed with Peppermint?

If you’re reading this, the refund has already been processed. Seriously, it's that fast!

Simply credit the customer’s card with a tap or do it remotely from the dashboard.

No waiting 7-30 days for the bank to process, no fees or penalties, no angry tweets from angry customers. Protect your brand with Peppermint.

For withdrawals, you decide. Hold funds within your business once a card is funded, allow customers move value between their physical and digital cards, and perhaps even to other Peppermint users.

Customize the withdrawal experience based on your risk profile and business objectives.

How do my customers top up their Peppermint cards?

Peppermint is the only offering in the market that allows customers top up their cards by themselves.

Customers simply fund yours or the Peppermint app and then tap their card to their phone to transfer value instantly.

No need to go to a specific location or agent; no need to use cash.

In-person and cash top-ups are nonetheless available with Peppermint. You simply provide agents or machine kiosks that your customers can visit to manage their cards.

Do I need a customer-facing app to issue Peppermint cards?

The choice is yours.

If you have tech-savvy customers and want to offer them an all-in-one experience, they can use the Peppermint app.

If you have an existing app, you can add the Peppermint plug-in, which will allow your customers manage their cards from your app. They can add value to their physical cards, deactivate cards, and even pay from their phone.

If you have neither and want to offer a card-only experience, you can do just that. Simply issue cards and manage your program from the dashboard.

The right choice will come down to your unique business operations and goals.

Meanwhile, the integration takes only a few minutes and we’ll be by your side the whole way ✨.

Are Peppermint cards the same as regular cards?

Peppermint cards are stored-value NFC cards, which are just as secure as regular bank cards, but with added reliability and convenience for making and accepting payments.
